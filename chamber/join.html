Membership Level Descriptions

Non Profit Membership

Description: Ideal for nonprofit organizations dedicated to community service and development.

Price: $200 annually

Benefits:

  • Access to networking events tailored for nonprofit professionals.
  • Visibility on the Chamber's website and promotional materials.
  • Participation in community outreach programs and volunteer opportunities.
  • Exclusive invitations to Chamber-sponsored workshops and seminars.

Bronze Membership

Description: Entry-level membership suitable for small businesses and startups.

Price: $500 annually

Benefits:

  • Basic listing in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Networking opportunities at Chamber-sponsored events and mixers.
  • Access to educational resources and workshops on business development.
  • Member discounts on Chamber-sponsored programs and services.

Silver Membership

Description: Mid-tier membership designed for growing businesses seeking enhanced visibility and support.

Price: $1000 annually

Benefits:

  • Enhanced listing in the Chamber's online directory with a detailed company profile.
  • Featured sponsorship opportunities at select Chamber events.
  • Complimentary admission to exclusive networking luncheons and business expos.
  • Access to specialized business training programs and mentorship opportunities.

Gold Membership

Description: Premier membership package offering maximum exposure and premium benefits.

Price: $2000 annually

Benefits:

  • Priority placement in the Chamber's online directory with premium branding options.
  • VIP invitations to exclusive networking receptions and industry roundtables.
  • Recognition as a top-tier sponsor at major Chamber events and galas.
  • Dedicated account management and personalized support from Chamber staff.
  • Complimentary access to premium business resources, including market research reports and strategic consultations.
